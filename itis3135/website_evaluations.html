Website Evaluations

Yale School of Art

This site is very cool as it's a wiki! Any student or staff of Yale is free to edit the webpage which I think is very unique for a college such as yale. This does however mean the design of the website is quite poor.

  • Contrast - There is most definitely some contrast here on this website. However, it is not good. There is too much contrast in too many places, it looks totally random and you have no idea what to look at.
  • Repetition - Not much repetition is happening on this website. The colors are all over the place, different pages have different background colors or pictures.
  • Alignment - The alignment on this page is atrocious. It is all over the place. Everything is inside of a grid/column, and it is still terrible alignment wise.
  • Proximity - At the very least, I would say the proximity of elements is quite good. There doesn't appear to be a place on the website where it's just random information mixed with other random information.

Boardwalk Insurance

Boardwalk Insurance has a very eye catching design. The orange really stands out as well as the hand drawn art on the page giving it a friendly feeling.

  • Contrast - The contrast on this website is excellent. The colors really pop, the font sizes make sense throughout the page, and the spacing between each element is good as well.
  • Repetition - Each webpage on this site follows the same exact colors, layout and style. They all also have the cute hand drawn elements, each page clearly feels like it's part of the same website
  • Alignment - Everything is placed carefully on this website. Nothing sticks out like a sore thumb. Everything is unified and aligned in a visually appealing way.
  • Proximity - The proximity is also great here. There is some spacing between similar elements with an increased spacing for unrelated elements, cluing you in that it's a different section.
